About Us

The association "WELCOMEHOME" was established in 1986 and is a non-profit organization, which aims to improve the protection of animals and promote their rights, including providing help to animals in distress, rehabilitating them and finding them adoptive homes. During the years of its existence, the association raised public awareness of the importance of protecting the lives of animals and preventing their abuse, through the expansion of the Animal Cruelty Law and its enforcement. The association, as part of its principles, does not kill animals that can be saved and works to reduce their deaths.
